About Me

Hi there! I'm Saad Mazhar, currently residing in the vibrant city of Colombo, Sri Lanka. I am deeply immersed in my educational journey, pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the Informatics Institute of Technology. My passion for technology has been a driving force, leading me to explore the dynamic world of computer science and its myriad applications. My academic foundation was laid at Isipathana College, where I not only excelled in my studies but also actively engaged in various clubs and societies. During my time there, I had the privilege of serving as the Captain of the English Debate Team and the President of the Interact Club of Isipathana College. These experiences honed my leadership and communication skills, shaping my path toward becoming a confident and effective individual. One of my favorite quotes that inspires me both in tech and life is, "Embrace the unknown; it's where the best stories unfold." This philosophy fuels my curiosity and motivates me to delve into the ever-evolving tech landscape, constantly seeking opportunities to learn, grow, and innovate. As a seasoned compere, I have hosted numerous events within my school, honing my public speaking abilities and fostering connections with diverse audiences. Feel free to reach out to me at saadmazaa@gmail.com. I am always eager to connect, collaborate, and engage in meaningful conversations related to technology, life, and everything in between.
